Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jennifer Connelly was born in Cairo, New York, and began her career as a child model. She made her acting debut in the 1984 film “Once Upon a Time in America.” Her breakthrough role came in 1986 when she starred in the fantasy film “Labyrinth.”
