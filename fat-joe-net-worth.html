Fat Joe | Career

Joe began his musical career as a member of the hip hop group Diggin’ in the Crates Crew.

He flourished in his career by establishing his own record label and signing fellow independent musicians to it.

Big musicians including DJ Khaled, Big Pun, Tony Sunshine, and others were signed to Fat Joe’s Terror Squad label.

Under the alias Fat Joe da Gangsta, he released his debut album “Represent” in 1993, followed by “Jealous One’s Envy” two years later.

In his second attempt, he deleted da Gangsta from his moniker and went by Fat Joe.

The album debuted at No. 71 on the Billboard Hot 200, and he was soon accepted by the rap world, landing a massive deal with Atlantic Records.
