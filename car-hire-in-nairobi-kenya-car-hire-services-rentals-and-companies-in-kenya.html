Car Hire in Kenya

Car hirein Kenya rents automobiles for short periods of time, generally ranging from a few hours to a few weeks. Car hire in Kenya are often organized with numerous local branches and primarily located near airports or busy city areas and often complemented by a website allowing online reservations. Car rentals in Kenya offer a variety of insurance packages/mileage options – so you can choose the rental conditions that suit your needs.
Alongside the basic rental of a vehicle, car hire companies in Kenya typically also offer extra products such as insurance, global positioning system (GPS) navigation systems, entertainment systems, and even such things as mobile phones.

Car Hire Services in Kenya

You can hire cars in Kenya with a driver (chauffeur-driven) or on self-drive basis. Car hire companies in Nairobi  offer salons, 4×4, and safari cars. Carefully read the rental contract to check for rules on insurance liabilities in case of accident or theft of the vehicle.
