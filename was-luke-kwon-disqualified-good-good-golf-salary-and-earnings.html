Luke Kwon Career Earnings As A Golfer

Luke Kwon has earned plenty as a professional golf player. As per pgatour.com, his total career earnings are around $789,558.

Kwon became a professional golfer in 2016. After three years, he joined PGA TOUR China in 2019 and Korn Ferry Tour in 2020. Aside from that, he also played at the University of Oklahoma as a golfer and was a team member with Max McGreevy and Charlie Saxon, PGA TOUR Series-China players.

Moreover, in the 2018 season, he stole the light in public; when he participated in the Macau Championship. However, he got left three shots behind Nick Voke, the winner at the time. Further, in the 2019 season, Kwon participated in many tournaments like Zhuzhou Classic, Huangshan Championship, Nantong Championship, Qinhuangdao Championship, Chongqing Championship, and more.

Likewise, in the 2019 season, the golfer played in five PGA TOUR Latinoamerica tournaments with 16 matches. Meanwhile, in the 2022 season, he was in action in around 10 PGA TOUR Latinoamerica tournaments.
