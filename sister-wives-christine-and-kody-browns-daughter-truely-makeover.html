Sister Wives' Christine and Kody Brown's daughter Truely, 12, shocks show fans with makeover includi

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, July 1, 2024

CHRISTINE Brown's daughter Truely has shocked fans with a major makeover.

The Sister Wives star posted a family photo on Instagram celebrating her daughter Gwendlyn Brown's engagement, but all eyes were on her other daughter Truely.

Truely, 12, went punk rock glam for the event, wearing a sheer black button-up shirt with a white tank top underneath, and a purple-colored skirt.

The sleeves of the sheer shirt gave the illusion of the pre-teen wearing tattoos on her arms.

Her hair, now dyed blue, poked out underneath a gray ski cap.

The 12-year-old finished off the look with skeleton-patterned black leggings.

Fans praised her look in the Instagram comments, with one writing: "As an ex-elder emo I approve of Truelys outfit tenfold."

Another wrote: Truely is a whole mood. I love it," while another praised: "Truely has main character energy."

"Truely needs her own show. She's the real star here!" wrote another.

Her reality star mom, Christine, 50, stood behind her wearing a figure-hugging pink sweater dress as she celebrated her sister Gwendlyn's engagement.

ENGAGEMENT PARTY

Christine captioned the photo: "What an awesome weekend spent with my daughters! We went to Gwen’s & Beas Engagement party and it was so wonderful to celebrate them."

Gwendlyn came out as bisexual in 2020 and is engaged to Beatriz Queiroz, who opted to wear all-black attire to their celebration.

Last month, Gwendlyn suggested that her parents' reality show, Sister Wives, had used her sexuality to try and boost ratings.

Christine and Kody's daughter admitted she felt "exploited" while on the show.

In a YouTube video, she slammed the show for forcing her to discuss her sexual preferences, even as her parents were in the middle of a bitter divorce.

In a recap of an October episode of Sister Wives, Gwendlyn confessed: "I look so mad in the moment, and I wasn't even upset. I was a little disappointed.

"I thought we were going to talk about the divorce. And all of the sudden, they were like, 'We're going to talk about your sexuality, Gwendlyn.'"

Gwendlyn has said her reaction videos provide a "unique perspective to my family's lifestyle as an autistic bisexual."

Watching the October episode, and reliving that period of her life, the reality daughter said she felt used.

Christine shares six children with ex Kody: Aspyn, 27; Mykelti, 26; Paedon, 24; Gwendlyn, 21; Ysabel, 19; and Truely, 12.

A NEW CHAPTER

Christine was the first of the wives to leave Kody, making the decision back in 2021 after 25 years together.

In a post on Instagram at the time, Christine confirmed the split and said: "After more than 25 years together, Kody and I have grown apart and I have made the difficult decision to leave.

"We will continue to be a strong presence in each other’s lives as we parent our beautiful children and support our wonderful family.

"At this time, we ask for your grace and kindness as we navigate through this stage within our family. With love, Christine Brown.”

Kody's third wife, Janelle, then followed suit in December 2022 and his first wife, Meri, announced their divorce last month.

Robyn is the only wife remaining, though Kody has 18 children with his wives in total.

She recently flaunted her slimmed-down new look paired with a declarative announcement on Instagram.
