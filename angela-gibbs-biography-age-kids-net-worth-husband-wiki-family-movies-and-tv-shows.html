Angela Gibbs Biography/ Wiki

Angela Gibbs Early Career

Gibbs is a talented woman who has been in the industry for over two decades and continues to work and promote young talents. Some of her greatest performances as an actress include her roles in Think Like a Man in 2012 and Think Like a man too in 2014, Isolation, Fled, Love Jacked, Together Brothers, Passing Glory to name a few.

Apart from being in the movies, Angela often appears on TV Screens, and some of her works include Suits, On my block, The Bricks, CSI, Grey’s Anatomy, Sealab 2021, and American Crime Story. Additionally, she won the HBO Short Award for Ties that Bind, and she was one of the coaching crews for the movie, The Revenant and casting director of the Last Breeze of Summer.

Angela is also the writer for the scenes of Awards show Trumpet Awards in 2002, 2003 and 2004. Also, she directed short movies and documentaries like Bitter Inheritance, Dare to Struggle/Dare to win to name a few.
