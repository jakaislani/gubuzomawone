See Kim Kardashians Flawless Wedding Hair &amp; Makeup

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

The photo, while black and white, clearly shows Kim's flawless wedding makeup: it's natural-looking and enhances all her best facial features. And while Mario created her makeup look, it was Jennifer Aniston's longtime hairstylist Chris McMillan who did her fabulous hair.

Everyone was wondering if Kim would end up going for an updo or wear her hair down for the wedding, and we have to say, we love that she chose to wear her hair down and showcase her gorgeous length.

Kim wore her hair parted down the center with just a subtle amount of romantic wave and movement, and like her makeup, we love this look on her. It was classic and beautiful, without taking any attention away from her Givenchy dress.
