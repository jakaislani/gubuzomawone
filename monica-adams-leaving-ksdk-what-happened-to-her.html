Is Monica Adams Leaving KSDK?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Is Monica Adams leaving KSDK? Fans and viewers have been wondering after her recent drop in appearances.

The award-winning television and radio host works as the morning show anchor for KSDK.

She is a proficient Journalist, reporter, host, anchor, writer, and producer.

Besides her journalism career, Monica is a Fitness Expert (C.P.T.), Life Coach, and Fashion Consultant.

Is Monica Adams Leaving KSDK? What Happened To Her?

Longtime Anchor Monica Adams is leaving the KSDK and, apparently, TV news.

Monica said her last day at the station would be Friday, January 13, but she appeared to have already disappeared from the Tegna station.

She shared her plans on her social media, seemingly taking a bit of a swipe at the station with the hashtag “#knowyourvalue.”

Monica had headed to the NBC-affiliated station after spending 15 years at KTVI.

“Today is the beginning of an incredible new chapter!” she posted on Facebook.

She mentioned that she had been preparing for this day her entire life. She thanked everyone for their support and invited them on her new journey.

Although she did not explicitly mention her plans, Monica appeared to have been discontent with her former work and extending towards a new career path.

Where Is Monica Adamas Now?

Outside of broadcasting, Monica Adams is a personal transformation specialist with expertise in Sports Medicine and Physical Therapy.

She has been a personal trainer for 24 years, making programs for several corporations and holding private events.

The multitalented lady is a design and wardrobe consultant passionate about style and design.

She specializes in men’s fashion with Bespoke and Bachrach Menswear in Clayton, Missouri. 

Likewise, she is a consultant and stylist for women with Claire Flowers.

Monica is married to James Quentin, Lt. with the St Louis County Police. The couple is yet to have a child. 

They love to travel, enjoy food and art, give back to the community, and spend time with the three dogs they have rescued.

Monica Adams Has Spent Decades In Reporting And Journalism

Monica Adams worked as a News Director in radio for WIL, KJCF/KTJJ, and KDHR/KHAD for 12 years. She also hosted a health and fitness show on KMOX radio in St. Louis for eight years.

The radio host served as an Assistant Sports Director, reporting on the St Louis Blues, Cardinals, and Rams.

Moreover, Monica was a part of a CMA award-winning morning team for WIL radio for eight years.

Afterward, she transitioned to television in 2005, working for FOX affiliate KTVI with roles in entertainment, fitness and health, traffic, and reporting.

Most recently, Monica served as the Morning Anchor on KSDK television, an NBC affiliate in St. Louis.

While at KSDK, she created two successful series, Monica’s Motivational Moments and Monica In The Metro.

She produced, wrote, and edited pieces covering all aspects of wellness during and after the pandemic.

Monica also started her entertainment pieces, which aired from 2000-2022, where she presents current movies at the box office and highlights A-list actors. 

Similarly, she has written extensively for Travelhost and Streetscape Magazine, penning lifestyle pieces and showcasing facets of St. Louis and its history.

Moreover, Monica is exceptionally charitably minded and has worked with multiple non-profit organizations.

She has also served as a brand spokesperson, emcee, motivational speaker, and auctioneer for almost three decades.

Monica has been honored with several awards, such as the Missouri Broadcaster’s AIR awards, and has been part of Emmy wins for both FOX and NBC.
